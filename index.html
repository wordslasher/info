🌟 WordSlasher – Our Story

Hi! We’re a passionate team of developers and language lovers on a mission to make learning fun, free, and accessible to everyone.

We’re building WordSlasher, an educational app that combines mini-games with real language learning — currently supporting Arabic, Spanish, and Japanese.

🎮 Learn by Playing

Imagine slicing through words like Fruit Ninja — but instead of fruit, you're learning vocabulary, sentence-building, and pronunciation better and faster than Duolingo.

No ads. No paywalls. No pressure.
Just joyful learning through immersive gameplay.

🌱 What Makes WordSlasher Different?

While big apps focus on streaks and gamified repetition, we’re focused on real comprehension — with beautiful design, cultural context, and games that make learning feel natural and exciting.

And we’re making the full demo 100% free, because we believe education should never be locked behind a paywall.
